# Decision 002: Committee Framework Initialization

**Proposal:** 002-Committee Framework Initialization  
**Date:** [Insert Date]  
**Decided by:** Governance Council  

## Summary
After review of Proposal 002, the Governance Council has approved the establishment of the initial committee framework for PEMTSbrothers. This framework defines the types, responsibilities, and operational guidelines for committees within the organization.

## Decision
1. **Committee Types Established**
   - **Executive Committee:** Oversees overall governance and ensures alignment with organizational vision.  
   - **Technical Committee:** Guides technical infrastructure, standards, and project implementations.  
   - **Advisory Committee:** Provides strategic advice, mentorship, and knowledge sharing.  
   - **Audit & Review Committee:** Monitors compliance, reviews proposals, and ensures institutional memory is accurate.

2. **Responsibilities**
   - Committees will operate according to their defined scope, using templates and workflows outlined in the governance repository.  
   - Decisions made by committees must be documented, reviewed, and, where necessary, escalated to the Governance Council.  

3. **Operational Guidelines**
   - Each committee will have a chairperson and designated members.  
   - Committees must follow the review and decision-making workflow as established in PEMTSbrothers governance.  
   - All actions must prioritize calm, transparency, and long-term utility over speed or authority.

4. **Implementation**
   - The committee framework is effective immediately.  
   - Members will be appointed in alignment with the organizationâ€™s principles of mentoring, service, and ethical governance.  

## Rationale
- Establishing committees ensures clarity of responsibilities and sustainable governance practices.  
- Framework allows growth without compromising calm, ethical, and useful systems.  
- Aligns with the spirit of Proposal 002 and the broader goals of PEMTSbrothers.
