# Decision 003: Committee Role Definition

## Decision
Approved

## Date
2026-02-01

## Reference
Proposal 003: Committee Role Definition (Advisory-Only Model)

## Decision Summary
PEMTSbrothers formally adopts an advisory-only model for all committees.

Committees are empowered to research, reflect, and recommend, but hold no execution or binding authority.

## Rationale
This decision protects institutional calm, prevents authority drift, and preserves transparency during early governance formation.

## Effect
- Committees operate as advisory bodies only
- All execution authority remains with documented governance decisions
- Any future change requires a new proposal and decision

Active
