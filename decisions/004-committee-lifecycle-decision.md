# Decision: Proposal 004 – Committee Lifecycle and Creation Process

## Proposal Reference
Proposal 004: Committee Lifecycle and Creation Process

## Decision Date
2026-02-04

## Decision Summary
Proposal 004 is **approved**. Committees must follow a formal lifecycle:

1. **Creation:** Only after proposal submission and review approval.
2. **Role Limitation:** Committees have advisory authority unless explicitly empowered.
3. **Duration:** Committees have a defined lifespan; renewal requires review.
4. **Transparency:** All actions must be logged in the institutional memory.
5. **Dissolution:** Committees must be dissolved upon completion or inactivity.

## Alignment
- Aligns with Proposal 002 and Decision 003
- Maintains PEMTSbrothers’ principle: *“we did not bring anything into this world, and we will not take anything out.”*
- Supports calm, ethical, and auditable governance
