# Decision 005 – Mentorship and Advisory Onboarding

**Proposal Decided:** 005 – Mentorship and Advisory Onboarding  
**Date:** [Insert Date]  
**Decision Maker(s):** [Insert Committee or Individual(s)]

## Decision
The proposal is **approved** as submitted with the following implementation directives:

1. All mentor and advisor onboarding must follow the process described in Proposal 005.  
2. Orientation materials must reference governance principles and institutional memory.  
3. Annual feedback and engagement reports must be submitted to the institutional memory repository.  
4. A designated point of contact will be assigned for each new mentor/advisor to ensure smooth onboarding.  
5. Adjustments to the process may occur in future proposals, but all changes must remain consistent with PEMTSbrothers governance principles.

**Rationale:**  
This decision ensures that mentorship and advisory activities are aligned with institutional governance, remain auditable, and support long-term stability and accountability within PEMTSbrothers.
