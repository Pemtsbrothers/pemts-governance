# Decision 006 – Mentorship and Advisory Workflow

## Proposal Decided
- Proposal 006 – Mentorship and Advisory Workflow

## Decision Summary
After reviewing Proposal 006 and considering Review 006, it is decided to adopt the proposed mentorship and advisory workflow for PEMTSbrothers.

### Key Points
1. Mentorship sessions and advisory activities will follow the documented schedule and workflow.
2. All session logs, guidance notes, and reports will be stored in the institutional memory repository (`pemts-memory`) for audit and reference.
3. Templates from `pemts-templates` will be used consistently to ensure standardization.
4. Governance oversight will occur quarterly, with exceptions documented and reviewed in future cycles.
5. Roles and responsibilities are clarified according to the advisory-only committee decisions (Decision 003).

## Implementation
- Mentorship and advisory activities may start immediately under the approved workflow.
- Any adjustments or optimizations will be proposed as new proposals in future governance cycles.

## Governance Alignment
This decision upholds PEMTSbrothers principles: calm, ethical, auditable, and service-oriented systems.
