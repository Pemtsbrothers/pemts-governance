## Decision Summary
After reviewing Proposal 007 and Review 007, the governance body approves the implementation of the technical automation workflows to support PEMTSbrothers operations.  

The implementation must follow these principles:

1. Automation must be **auditable and reversible**.
2. Human oversight is **mandatory** for all automated actions impacting governance, decisions, or institutional memory.
3. Fallback procedures are defined for all critical automation steps.
4. Implementation will be incremental, with regular reviews for adjustments and corrections.
5. Maintain alignment with PEMTSbrothers’ core values: **calm, ethical, and service-oriented operations**.
