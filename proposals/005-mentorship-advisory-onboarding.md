## Purpose
To define the process for onboarding mentors and advisors into PEMTSbrothers committees and advisory bodies.

## Proposal
1. **Application:** Interested mentors/advisors submit a formal application referencing skills, availability, and advisory experience.
2. **Review:** Applications reviewed by the Technical Committee and Advisory Board.
3. **Approval:** Successful candidates are formally added to committees or advisory bodies with defined roles.
4. **Onboarding:** Orientation includes governance principles, code of conduct, and institutional memory overview.
5. **Evaluation:** Annual review of participation and contribution; continuation requires active engagement.

## Alignment
- Supports Decisions 002, 003, and 004.
- Ensures calm, ethical, and auditable mentorship practices.
- Preserves institutional knowledge while enabling external guidance.
