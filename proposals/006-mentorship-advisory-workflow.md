# Proposal 006 – Mentorship and Advisory Workflow

## Purpose
Define the operational workflow for mentorship and advisory engagements within PEMTSbrothers. Ensure alignment with governance, review procedures, and institutional memory tracking.

## Scope
- Mentorship sessions and advisory interactions.
- Reporting and documentation requirements.
- Integration with institutional memory for audits.

## Workflow
1. **Assignment**
   - Each mentor/advisor is assigned a mentee group or advisory task.
   - Point of contact from the governance committee is designated.

2. **Orientation**
   - All participants complete onboarding following approved orientation materials.
   - Orientation checklists must be completed and archived.

3. **Session Scheduling**
   - Sessions are scheduled on a monthly or as-needed basis.
   - Updates logged in institutional memory repository.

4. **Documentation**
   - Session notes are submitted in the standard template (`pemts-templates`).
   - Feedback forms collected for mentees and mentors.

5. **Review & Audit**
   - Governance committee reviews reports quarterly.
   - Any adjustments follow Proposal → Review → Decision pattern.

## Expected Outcomes
- Transparent and auditable mentorship operations.
- Consistent alignment with PEMTSbrothers governance principles.
- Clear documentation for all participants.
