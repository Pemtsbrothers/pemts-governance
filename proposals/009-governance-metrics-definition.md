# Proposal 009 – Governance Metrics Definition

## Purpose
Define a clear framework to measure the effectiveness, transparency, and ethical alignment of governance operations in PEMTSbrothers.

## Background
As PEMTSbrothers grows, we need quantitative and qualitative indicators to ensure decisions remain calm, fair, and aligned with our core values.

## Proposed Metrics
1. **Decision Timeliness** – Average time between proposal submission and decision.
2. **Review Completeness** – Percentage of proposals that undergo full review cycles.
3. **Stakeholder Satisfaction** – Feedback collected from committees and contributors.
4. **Adherence to Values** – Audit of decisions for alignment with calmness, kindness, and long-term usefulness.

## Implementation
- Metrics to be reviewed quarterly.
- Results will be recorded in institutional memory.
- Adjustments to the framework may be proposed as needed.
